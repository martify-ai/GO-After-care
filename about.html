About Us

Compassion at the heart of palliative care.

Our Vision

Extending palliative care services to the grassroots, ensuring no one faces life-threatening illness alone.

Our Mission

To deliver community-based palliative care and foster partnerships for advocacy, training, and research.

Our Story

Founded in 2023, Go-After Care Initiative was born out of a need to bridge the gap in palliative care accessibility. Our team of healthcare professionals and volunteers work tirelessly to bring comfort to patients and families in underserved communities.
